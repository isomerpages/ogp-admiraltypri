<h4>Objectives</h4>
<p><strong>Vision</strong>
</p>
<p>Nurturing Passionate Learners in Mother Tongue Language
<br>We aim to inculcate the love for Malay Language in our pupils so that
they can be effective users of the language and to uphold the Malay heritage
and cultural values. Pupils will be confident speakers and be able to interact
with others effectively using the MT language. They will be self-directed
learners with perseverance in achieving excellence for the language and
stay strong rootedness in own culture and understanding the diversity of
other cultures and respecting them.
<br>
<br>The teaching and learning of Malay Language also emphasize the infusion
of ICT, apart from other printed resources. Students need to be given the
opportunity to explore a repertoire of resources to access knowledge and
use ICT in their teaching and learning activities such as during presentation
of projects.</p>
