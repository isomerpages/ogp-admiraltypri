<h4>Additional info</h4>
<p><strong>FAQs:</strong>
</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Does my child need to have any musical knowledge in order to join the
Guzheng Ensemble?
<br>
</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>No. Every new member will be taught the basics eg. note-reading, music scales, etc. in their first year of joining the Guzheng Ensemble.</strong>
</p>
<ol start="2" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Does my child need to buy a Guzheng?</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>We strongly encourage members to purchase their own Guzheng so that they
will take greater ownership of their learning, and practice daily.</p>
<p><strong>Students are required to purchase their own Guzheng Picks and textbook.</strong>
</p>
<ol start="3" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>How can I better support my child in his/ her learning to play the Guzheng?
<br>
</p>
</li>
</ol>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Encourage him/ her to practice at home what he/ she has learnt during
CCA sessions. We recommend our members to practice daily.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Offer him/ her words of encouragement whenever he/ she is struggling to
master a certain skill or song. Allow him/ her to use additional online
resources, eg. tutorial videos from YouTube or other web-sites. For e-CCA,
all online lesson will be recorded and posted on Google Classroom.</p>
</li>
</ul>
