<h4>Goals</h4>
<p>To promote and develop students’ interest and competency in sports to
enable them to lead an active and healthy lifestyle.</p>
<h4>Objectives</h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Competent in playing the sports taught in CCA</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Transference of skills learnt across sports taught in CCA.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Able to work with different teams and build team spirit</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Able to develop leadership skills</p>
</li>
</ul>
