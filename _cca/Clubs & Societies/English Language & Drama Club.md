<p>The English Language &amp; Drama Club provides opportunities for pupils
to develop a lifelong passion for the language through games and drama.
Besides engaging in mindful play like Scrabble, students also learn about
the various drama techniques of vocal variety, voice projection, facial
and spatial awareness, tableau, improvisation etc.</p>
<p>Students are also involved in stagecraft; staging and production processes
that include scriptwriting, stage managing (use of space and behind the
scenes work e.g., sounds and lighting) and directing. Students’ knowledge
on stagecraft will be deepened through learning journeys to MediaCorp /
Esplanade / Wild Rice Theatre as they get to experience first-hand, what
goes on behind the scenes of these theatres.</p>

<h4><strong>Objectives</strong></h4>
<p>Pupils will be able to:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Acquire acting and stagecraft (scriptwriting, stage managing &amp; directing)
skills</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Explore, experiment and express themselves using the drama techniques</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develop social and emotional competencies while working together to achieve
a common creative goal</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Apply strategic thinking and decision-making skills during competitions,
performances, or activities carried out during CCA.</p>
</li>
</ol>
