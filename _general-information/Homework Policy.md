<h1>Homework Policy</h1><p><strong>Purposes of Homework</strong></p><p>Homework is an important component of the teaching-learning process. It aims to serve the following purposes:</p><p>a. stimulate thinking and develop ideas, initiative and creativity</p><p>b. reinforce and deepen understanding of lessons taught</p><p>c. allow students to regularly practise, and apply skills and concepts taught</p><p>d. inculcate responsibility for independent learning and encourage partnership with parents</p><p>e. encourage partnership with parents</p><p><strong>Types of Homework</strong></p><p>Homework should be varied in type in accordance with the needs of pupils and the objectives of the lessons. There can be different types of homework for a variety of purposes. Common examples are:</p><p>a. Practice Exercises:</p><ul data-tight="true" class="tight"><li><p>Consolidation exercise e.g. maths, memorization of tables</p></li><li><p>Practising for mastery e.g. spelling words</p></li><li><p>Revising information about a current topic</p></li><li><p>Reading for pleasure</p></li><li><p>Essay writing</p></li><li><p>Drawing of pictures and diagram to summarize ideas and theories</p></li></ul><p>b. Preparatory Homework:</p><ul data-tight="true" class="tight"><li><p>Background reading</p></li><li><p>Researching topics for a class unit of work</p></li><li><p>Collecting items/ Gather data e.g. geometric shapes.</p></li></ul><p>c. Extension Assignments:</p><ul data-tight="true" class="tight"><li><p>Making or designing something e.g. an art work</p></li><li><p>Investigations e.g. Science, Social Studies, etc.</p></li><li><p>Researching e.g. history, local news</p></li><li><p>Collaborative Assignments like project work IT-based Homework</p></li></ul><p><strong>Recommended Homework Time</strong></p><p>The table below shows the guide for homework time.</p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Recommended Home Work Time" src="/images/HomeworkTable.jpg"></div><p><strong>Communication with Parents/Guardians</strong></p><ul data-tight="true" class="tight"><li><p>Homework may be recorded by the pupil in the Pupilsâ€™ Companion and messages can be recorded by the teacher(s) and parents as well.</p></li><li><p>Parents are requested to inform the teacher if their children are having difficulties with the homework given.</p></li><li><p>If homework cannot be completed within the estimated duration of time given, do allow some extra time of about 10 mins.
